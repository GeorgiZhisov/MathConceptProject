Browser Game • Finite State Machine

Prison Escape FSM

Stealth • Prison Break • Guard AI

Escape the prison without getting caught.

Explore during the day. At night the guards switch to full FSM behavior: PATROL, SUSPICIOUS, SEARCH, CHASE, and RETURN.

Day / Night Plan by day, escape by night
Guard AI Independent 5-state FSM guards
Stealth Vision, hearing, alerts, hiding spots
Objective Reach the final gate before losing all lives

Game Mode

Easy = fewer guards. Hard = faster guards. Roam = prison tour with no guards.

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys — move
  • Shift — walk slower / quieter
  • E — interact with cabinets, boards, switches, escapes
  • I or Tab — open inventory
  • E — interact or use selected inventory item
  • ESC — pause
  • M — mute audio
  • R — restart run

Tip: hide before a guard fully commits to a chase.

Break Time

Mission Briefing

How to play

  1. Leave your cell during the day and learn the prison.
  2. Watch where guards patrol and remember safe hiding spots.
  3. You have 5 days to escape. Each day and night lasts 60 seconds.
  4. Search cabinets with E to find contraband like lockpicks, keycards, bottles, cutters, and disguises. Open the inventory with I to see item uses.
  5. Two rooms are keycard-locked. The vent and vehicle routes now lead into separate chase maps.
  6. Lose all 3 lives and the run ends. Reach the gate to win.
